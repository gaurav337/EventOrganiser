About Us

Founded in 2014, our team specializes in creating unforgettable experiences for nonprofit fundraisers and social gatherings. With a passion for excellence and a collaborative approach, we strive to exceed client expectations, providing stress-free events.

Based in Kansas City, we proudly serve clients nationwide and internationally, offering tailored event solutions to meet diverse needs.

We are dedicated to creating memorable events that you and your guests will cherish forever. With years of experience in the industry, our team of professionals is here to make your event special.

Time for a backstory

Chappelow Events was founded in 2014 by Beth Chappelow. Over the past decade, Chappelow Events has grown to become one of the leading planning and design companies in Kansas City and beyond. From coast to coast and abroad, our team’s greatest pleasure is exceeding client expectations.
